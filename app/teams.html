How teams use it

Three moves that kill bad decisions early.

The same engine that trains competitive debaters runs the adversarial thinking your team already knows you should be doing but never actually schedules.

01

Red-team any proposal

Describe the plan in plain English. AI runs the strongest opposition case — the argument your toughest board member would make, with specifics and a warrant chain. Find the hole before they do.

02

Pre-mortem drills

"It's 12 months out. The launch failed. Why?" AI walks the failure modes ranked by likelihood — execution, market timing, competitor response, internal politics — and flags which ones your current plan doesn't defend against.

03

Stakeholder rehearsal

Run the next meeting against an AI playing your CFO, the most skeptical VP, or the customer who might churn. Spot the one question that tanks the plan. Rehearse the answer until it lands.
