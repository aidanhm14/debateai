AI debate practice routine

Pick one motion and write the central clash in one sentence.
Give a two-minute constructive speech with one argument and one example.
Ask the AI for the strongest rebuttal, then answer only that rebuttal.
Get judge feedback on weighing, logic, evidence, and clarity.

AI debate practice FAQ

Can AI replace real debate practice?

No. It is best for repetition, drilling, and feedback between live rounds.

What formats can I practice?

Use it for parliamentary, public forum, Lincoln-Douglas, policy, school motions, model UN speeches, and general argument drills.

How do I improve fastest?

Do shorter rounds, isolate one skill, and review the same mistake until it stops appearing.
