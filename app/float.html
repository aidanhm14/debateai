What's in the floating window

Everything you need to stay in the round without switching back to the tab.

  • Live transcript. The latest spoken line, so you can follow while you work.
  • Speech clock. The current speech and time remaining.
  • One-tap POI. Cut in to make a point. It pauses the AI and unmutes you.
  • Mute and end. Full control of the round from the float.

Browser support

Chrome and Edge

The round floats in a real always-on-top window (Document Picture-in-Picture) that stays visible over your other desktop apps, even when the browser is minimized.

Firefox and Safari

Document Picture-in-Picture is not available yet, so the round docks to a corner panel that floats over the site. The audio still keeps running in the background tab.

Want a one-click launcher?

The floating works on its own, in the round, with no install. For a toolbar button that opens a round from anywhere, there is an optional Chrome / Edge launcher extension. A Web Store version is coming; for now you can load it unpacked.

Load the launcher (developer install)

  1. Open chrome://extensions (or edge://extensions).
  2. Turn on Developer mode, top-right.
  3. Click Load unpacked and select the float-extension folder from the DebateIt repo.
  4. Pin the icon and click it to open a floating round.

The extension is just a launcher. The floating itself is done by the round (Document Picture-in-Picture), so you do not need the extension to use background mode.
