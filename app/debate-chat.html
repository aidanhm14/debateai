    FAQ

    Do I need to sign in?

    Yes. One tap with Google. Sign-in is what lets the matchmaker pair two specific people and keep the queue safe from bots. We don't post anything, we don't email you, and there's no card on file.

    How long is the wait?

    Depends on who's online. On busy evenings it's seconds; on a quiet Tuesday afternoon it can be a few minutes. There's no AI fallback on this page — you wait or you cancel.

    How is this different from /spar?

    /spar gates pairings by debate format (APDA, BP, PF, etc.) and falls back to an AI partner after 60s. This page is the opposite: no format, humans only, the prompt is the whole structure.

    How is this different from a general AI chatbot?

    There's a real person on the other side. The whole product is built for arguing out loud against someone who'll push back. Built by a national APDA champion at UChicago for people who actually care about format and clash.

    Is the video private?

    The room is a one-off video URL that expires after 24 hours. Only the two paired users have the URL. We don't record. Full privacy policy →

    What if my opponent is rude?

    Leave the room. Come back to this page and find a new one. If something is seriously wrong, email feedback@debateai.com with the room ID.
