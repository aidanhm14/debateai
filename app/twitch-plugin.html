How to use it, start to ballot

  • 1. Type the channel name above and press Open. The judge console opens in a new view.
  • 2. Press Start listening in the console. Your browser asks what to share: pick the tab or window playing the stream and tick "Also share tab audio."
  • 3. Watch the flow fill in as people speak. Chat from the channel streams in as audience signal.
  • 4. Ask the judge for a live read any time, and press End and write ballot when the round is over.

What it reads

  • Stream or room audio through browser capture.
  • Public Twitch chat, when a channel is provided.
  • Host notes such as format, motion, and judge paradigm.

Chat is not a ballot

Chat tells the judge where the audience is confused or persuaded. It never decides who won. The ballot comes from the flow.

Broadcaster mode

Run the judge from a dashboard tab and capture stream audio. Keep voice guidance muted unless you deliberately route it into broadcast audio.
