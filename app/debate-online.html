Most "online debate" tools are forum threads, chat with a general AI, or asynchronous comment battles. This one is built for the sport. A real-time voice round on a clock, format-native structure, an opponent who pushes back, and a judge ballot scored on the criteria your format actually uses.

Online debate FAQ

Where can I debate online for free?

Right here. /spar pairs you with a real human. /debate-it and /voice-debate let you spar against AI. /live hosts scheduled human-vs-human tournament rounds. Free while in beta; future pricing is reference-only.

How do I debate online with strangers?

Open /spar, propose a format, hit Find. You're paired with the next debater on the queue. Pairing usually lands in under a minute when anyone else is online. No format gate, no AI fallback. It's the closest thing to Omegle for debate; the full walkthrough is at /debate-strangers.

How do I practice debate online if I don't have a partner?

Use /voice-debate. The AI takes the opposing side at varsity level, gives POIs, interrupts when relevant, and writes the judge ballot when you finish. You get a full round in under 10 minutes for casual formats, ~45 minutes for APDA, ~60 minutes for BP.

Which online debate platform is best for [my format]?

This one covers Asian Parliamentary, WSDC, BP, APDA, PF, LD, Policy, Congress, MUN, and a casual Quick Clash mode. Format selection is in /debate-it. Each format has its own speech timing, citation rules, judging criteria, and speaker scale baked into the AI's prompt.

Can I debate online without signing up?

Yes. Anonymous visitors get 5 AI rounds on /debate-it before the soft cap, and human pairing on /spar works as a guest too. Signing in with Google bumps the AI cap to 10 total and gives you a named profile in live rounds.

Is this an online debate tournament site?

Not yet. /live hosts individual scheduled rounds with AI judging. A flagship online tournament with AI prelims + human elims is on the year-out roadmap (see the goals section on the landing page).

Can I debate online in languages other than English?

Yes. The picker on the landing page sets the AI's output language across 14 options including Hindi, Spanish, French, Mandarin, Arabic, Portuguese, Bahasa Indonesia, and more. The AI replies in your language; the TTS synthesizes voice in the matching language too.

How is this different from a generic AI chat app?

Three things. Real-time voice round on a clock instead of asynchronous chat. Format-accurate structure (Policy gets tagged cards, LD gets value/criterion, BP gets extensions). And a judge ballot at the end with speaker points and a concrete next-round drill, not a "great points on both sides" summary.

Where can I debate with people online for free?

Right here. /spar pairs you with another debater on video, and /live hosts scheduled human-vs-human tournament rounds. An AI judge writes the ballot either way. Free while in beta.

Can I debate real people instead of an AI?

Yes. Human rounds are the point: hit Find on /spar for a live opponent, or organize a scheduled round on /live. The AI debates you only when you pick the AI path; in human rounds it just writes the judge ballot.
