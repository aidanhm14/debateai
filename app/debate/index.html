Debate Both Sides of the Big Questions

Each page below is a short AI debate on a question people are arguing right now. You get the strongest case on both sides, a judge's verdict, and a button to take a side and run the round yourself against an AI that pushes back.

AI & technology

AI
Should AI be regulated?
Safety and accountability versus innovation and the global race. Where the regulation line sits decides the round.
AI
Will AI replace human jobs?
Replacement versus reshaping, and whether retraining can keep pace with the speed of the shift.
AI · Education
Should students use AI in school?
A tutor for everyone, or a shortcut past the struggle that learning is made of.
AI · Law
Should AI art be copyrighted?
Human authorship versus machine output, and exactly where a prompt becomes a creative act.
Tech · Security
Should the US ban TikTok?
National-security threat versus a guaranteed speech cost on 150 million users. Ban or divest?
Tech · Youth
Ban social media for minors?
A developing-brain harm argument against a privacy and enforcement problem nobody has solved.

Economy & society

Economics
Is universal basic income a good idea?
An unconditional floor versus the cost of paying everyone, including those who don't need it.
Climate · Energy
Is nuclear energy worth the risk?
Firm, carbon-free power versus cost, delay, and the opportunity cost against renewables.
