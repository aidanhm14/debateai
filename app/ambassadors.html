The ground game

Debate grows one room at a time. Be the one who opens it.

DebateIt ambassadors are debaters who already live on a circuit. They run rounds where debaters already are, bring their own schools on, and help a teacher at a school with no program become its first coach. Paid work, real credit, and a title that carries.

We are recruiting the founding class: 30 ambassadors across the live circuits for the 2026-27 season.

What the job is ↓
The job

Three things, done well.

01 · SEED

Run rounds where debaters are

Practice nights, club meetings, the hallway between tournament rounds. You know the rooms. Bring the judge into them and let the ballot do the convincing.

02 · ONBOARD

Bring your school on

Set up your team's space, get your coach reading the ballots, make judged practice a weekly habit instead of a scarce event. Your program stops rationing rounds.

03 · MULTIPLY

Make the next coach

Most schools have no debate program because they have no one to run it. You walk a teacher or a parent through the online coach track; the AI judges every round, they run the room.

The ladder

Ambassador is the first rung.

The people on the ground now are the people we build around. The founding class is small on purpose.

Ambassador

Seeds rounds on a circuit, activates schools, gets paid per school brought on plus event work. Trained by us, credited by name.

→
Founding coach

Runs a program they helped start, supports the younger students, and refers the next school on the circuit.

→
Regional lead

Owns a region's growth, hires its next ambassadors, and runs its hosted events and tournaments.

Terms are agreed before you start, in writing. Schools and coaches can begin today; the product is free in beta at debateai.com.
