The role

DebateIt is the AI judge for spoken argument. Students debate out loud; it scores the round, decides the winner, and writes a tournament-style ballot in seconds. The product is live at debateai.com.

Debate's bottleneck has never been interest. It is judges and coaches. Most schools have no program because nobody is free to run one, and the schools that do have programs ration practice rounds around scarce judging. Ambassadors are how we fix that on the ground, one room at a time.

You already compete. This role is doing what you already do, with a paycheck and a title attached: running rounds, bringing your school on, and helping the next school start a program.

What you will do

  • Run judged rounds at your club's practices and at tournaments you already attend. The ballot does the convincing; you run the room.
  • Onboard your own team. Set up the team space, get your coach reading ballots, make judged practice a weekly habit instead of a scarce event.
  • Help one nearby school start a program. You walk a teacher, parent, or student through our coach track. The AI judges every round; they run the room.
  • Host one online event a season with our support: a scrimmage, a ladder night, a novice round-robin.
  • Tell us what breaks. Ambassador feedback sets the product roadmap. You have a direct line to the founding team.

Who we are looking for

  • You compete on a live circuit, high school or college, in any format.
  • People know you: your team, your coach, the teams you hit at tournaments.
  • You can run a room. Explain the rules, keep the clock, keep it fun.
  • You want receipts. A title, a reference from a funded company, and growth numbers with your name on them.

Pay and terms

  • Monthly stipend through the season, plus a bonus for every school you bring on.
  • Exact terms agreed in writing before you start. No commission-only mystery math.
  • Season runs September through April. Expect 3 to 5 hours a week, more during your own tournaments, which you were attending anyway.
  • Training from us: the online coach track, playbooks, and a working relationship with the founding team.

Where this goes

Ambassador› Founding coach› Regional lead

Ambassadors who bring schools on become founding coaches of the programs they started. Founding coaches who grow a region hire its next ambassadors. When we make full-time hires, this bench is where we look first.
