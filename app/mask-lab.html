Camera lab

The camera converter for live rounds. Three modes: camera on, anonymous avatar, camera off. Left is your raw camera (never leaves this page). Right is the converted feed, the only thing a debate room would ever transmit. In avatar mode the mouth moves with your voice and camera pixels are never drawn at all.
