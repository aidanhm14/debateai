Odds, rating, and point rules

Why 50% with zero bets?

Each side starts with 100 house points, so every new market begins at 50% and 2.00x. The first real prediction moves it.

Points are not rating

Points are what you place in the pool. Rating is your ladder score. Calling an underdog correctly moves rating more.

+30 +21 +12
20% underdog80% favorite

Free, virtual, and not cashable

Everyone starts with 1,000. Points cannot be bought, withdrawn, redeemed, converted, or transferred. The winner is decided by the AI judge, not a random draw.

Points are a scorekeeping device for reading arguments, with no cash value inside or outside the product. This is not a wagering service and nothing on this page is an offer to gamble. Balances can be reset or adjusted while Predict is in beta.
