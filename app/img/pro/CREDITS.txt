Profession-card photos on the landing page, in the "The skills travel" section.
All sourced 2026-07-01 from Pexels (pexels.com), replacing the earlier Wikimedia
public-domain set, which was all US-government press-pool photography: it read
as dated diplomatic/State-Dept scenes and put recognizable politicians (Kerry,
Lavrov, Blinken, Sullivan) on product marketing cards. The Pexels license
permits commercial use with no attribution required; provenance is recorded
here anyway so future edits can verify sources. Selection criteria: the scene
reads at a glance for each profession, no recognizable public figures, faces
incidental rather than portrait-like, and compositions that survive the wide
banner crop (.pro-photo, 196px tall). All files fetched as 1600x900 crops via
the Pexels CDN (fit=crop).
