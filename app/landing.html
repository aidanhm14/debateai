What You Get

Six tools inside one engine.

Each one does a specific thing. No filler features.

Case Generator

Enter a motion and side. Get a full case: framework, 3 arguments with warrant chains, compound impact, and A2 blocks for the top 5 opp responses. Quick mode for 15-min prep, deep mode for practice.

Input: motion + side. Output: structured case document.

Round Simulator

Practice any speech (PM, LO, MG, MO, LOR, PMR) against an AI opponent. It reads your arguments and responds with real counterarguments, not generic lines. Timed like a real round.

Input: your speech. Output: opponent's response + judge notes.

Philosophy Engine

50+ thinkers with period-accurate detail. Rawls: early vs. late, two principles, lexical ordering. Kant: all three formulations. Foucault: archaeology vs. genealogy. Not Wikipedia summaries — debate-ready depth.

Input: thinker or concept. Output: debate-applicable breakdown.

Judge Feedback

Paste a speech you've written. Get line-by-line feedback: where your warrants are thin, where your time allocation is off, where you dropped a key argument, and what a judge would actually write on a ballot.

Input: your speech text. Output: paragraph-level critique.

Strategic Mapper

Paste a motion and the mapper shows you the full strategic landscape: which arguments interact, where the real clash is, what opp will likely collapse on, and which points the judge weighs heaviest.

Input: motion + context. Output: argument interaction map.

Argument Market

Submit any claim. The engine generates 3 arguments for and 3 against, scores each on evidence and logic, then runs adversarial cycles where arguments attack each other. After 3–5 cycles, weak arguments die and strong ones survive.

Input: a claim. Output: ranked, stress-tested arguments.
How to Use It

Open it. Use it. That's it.

No account required to start. No setup. Works on desktop and mobile.

1

Pick a tool

Open the app and tap any of the six tabs. Case Gen, Round Sim, Philosophy, Judge, Mapper, or Market. Each one works independently.

2

Give it input

Enter a motion, paste a speech, type a claim, or pick a philosopher. Each tool tells you exactly what it needs. Most results come back in under 30 seconds.

3

Iterate or export

Revise the output, ask follow-up questions, copy to clipboard, or jump to another tool with the same motion. Your session saves automatically.

Pricing

Why there's a token system.

Every time you generate a case, simulate a round, or run any tool, it costs us real money. The AI models that power this aren't free — each request uses compute from Anthropic's Claude API.

The token system keeps the app sustainable. Instead of a flat monthly fee where light users subsidize heavy users, you pay for what you use. Generate 5 cases a month? Pay for 5. Generate 50? Pay for 50.

This means we can keep improving the engine without cutting corners on model quality. You get the best AI available, not a cheaper model to protect margins.

During beta, it's all free.

Every tool, unlimited usage, no credit card. We're testing with competitive debaters to make the product better before paid plans launch.
