Why voice-first matters
Reading an argument isn't the same as saying it.

The words you wrote sound different coming out of your mouth. The pushback lands harder when it's spoken at you in real time. The moment you realize you can't answer the follow-up — that's the rep. You do it here, not in the room that matters.
