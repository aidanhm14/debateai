The honest answers

What happens if I cancel?

You keep full paid access until the end of your current billing period, no lockout mid-tournament. After that, you drop back to the free plan (10 lifetime generations once signed in). One-click from the app's Team tab.

Why is this so cheap compared to other AI tools?

Because I'm a student, and debaters are students. The margins are fine, I use the same APIs ChatGPT Pro uses, but I'm not paying a marketing team. Price goes up if I ever take funding; current prices lock in for anyone who signs up now.

Can one subscription serve my whole team?

Individual covers 10 seats. Team covers 50. Invite via email from your Team tab; everyone on your team shares your request pool.

What if I don't use all 250 requests?

They don't roll over, it resets monthly. But if you're consistently under 50 requests, honestly BYOK at $1/mo or Lifetime at $14.99 is a better deal. The Individual plan is priced for people who actually hit 100+ drafts a month.

Does it actually make me better, or is it just another ChatGPT?

It's a debate engine, not a chatbot. Prompts are tuned by format (APDA / WSDC / PF / LD / Policy / Congress / BP), depth levels match round structure, Judge Analyzer reads real round logs, and every generation feeds back into prompt tuning. ChatGPT will generate you a persuasive blob. This will generate you a case your opp has to work to beat.

Is my data used to train models?

Your cases are stored in your account and used by us to improve the prompt library over time (anonymized). They are not sent to third-party training. You can delete your data anytime from the Team tab.
