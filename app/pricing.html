What happens if I cancel?

You keep full paid access until the end of your current billing period, no lockout mid-tournament. After that, you drop back to the free plan: 1 local preview, then 10 signed-in requests. One-click from the app's Team tab.

Why is this so cheap compared to other AI tools?

Because the first paid buyers are students, coaches, and clubs. The public plans are annual on purpose: they should feel closer to a tournament fee than a monthly software bill. Larger school, district, and business rollouts get scoped separately before we invent a fixed price.

Can one subscription serve my whole team?

Individual covers 10 seats. Team covers 50. Invite via email from your Team tab; everyone on your team shares your request pool.

I coach a team. What should I budget?

For one coach and a few students, Individual can be enough. For a real roster, budget around the Team plan: $50/year for 50 seats and 1,500 pooled monthly requests after beta. If you need more than that, email us with roster size and expected usage.

Do schools or districts get a different plan?

Not a fixed public tier yet. Schools usually start from Team. Districts and leagues need a quick scope first: number of schools, seat count, admin owner, privacy paperwork, and whether each program needs its own workspace.

Can businesses use DebateIt?

Yes. The same engine supports pitch defense, negotiation, courtroom, sales objections, and team red-teaming. Start with Team beta; if the buyer needs invoicing, admin reporting, or a custom rollout, email us before billing turns on.

Can we pay by invoice or purchase order?

For public plans, checkout will be self-serve once billing turns on. For schools, districts, and business teams, email us. We can handle the conversation before deciding whether invoice billing belongs in the first paid release.

What if I don't use all 250 requests?

They don't roll over, it resets monthly. At $10/year, Individual is priced to be a no-brainer even for occasional users. If you'd rather pay once and forget, Lifetime at $30 covers the same caps.

How will referral codes work?

First, as attribution. A referral URL should carry the code through sign-in so we can see who is bringing real users. After billing turns on, we can test credits for the new user and rewards for the referrer once a referred user becomes paid.

Does it actually make me better at debate?

It's a debate engine, not a general-purpose chat. The AI is tuned per format (APDA / WSDC / PF / LD / Policy / Congress / BP), so whatever you compete in, it speaks your rules. Depth levels match how long real speeches run, Judge Analyzer reads real round logs, and every round you run feeds back into the AI's next round. The output is a case your opponent has to actually work to beat.

Is my data used to train models?

Your cases are not sent to GPT, Claude, or any third-party foundation model for training. Signed-in, rated or saved work can feed DebateIt's internal learning loop, which sharpens our debate model for future rounds. You can delete your data anytime from the Team tab.
