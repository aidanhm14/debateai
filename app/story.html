Why this exists

I built a sparring partner because I needed one.

I started debating in middle school. I'm still doing it.

I loved it from the first round. The clash, the pressure, the moment a flow turns from defensive to offensive in real time. I kept finding the next tournament. High school nationals. APDA in college.

Prep was always the problem. It's a solo activity that needs two people. You can flow your own case all night. You can read articles until your eyes burn. None of that simulates the moment your opponent stands up and tells you your second contention contradicts your framework.

You need someone to argue against. Most nights you don't have one. The people who would take a round with you are sick of running drills for you.

So I built one.

A sparring partner that picks up at 11pm. Format-accurate across APDA, BP, WSDC, LD, PF, Policy, Asian Parli, Congress, MUN. Cuts in mid-sentence. Takes POIs. Writes a real ballot. Argues the other side because that's the side you haven't prepped.

If prep is your bottleneck, take it for a round.

Start a round Free in beta. No card.
