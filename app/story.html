Why this exists

I built a sparring partner because I needed one.

I started debating in middle school. I'm still doing it.

I loved it from the first round. The clash, the pressure, the moment a flow turns from defensive to offensive in real time. I kept finding the next tournament. High school nationals. APDA in college.

Prep was always the bottleneck. Debate is a solo activity that needs two people. You can flow your own case all night. You can read until your eyes burn. None of that is the moment an opponent stands up and tells you your second contention guts your own framework.

Four years of this taught me the best prep is war-gaming the meanest version of the other side. And I was still walking into rounds missing the attacks I was wired not to see.

You need someone to argue against. Most nights you don't have one, and the people who will take a round with you get sick of running drills for you.

So I built one.

A sparring partner that picks up at 11pm and argues the side you didn't prep. Format-accurate across APDA, BP, Worlds, LD, PF, Policy, Asian Parli, Congress, and MUN. It cuts in mid-sentence. It takes POIs. It writes a real ballot.

I built it for me. It turned out a few thousand other people needed the same thing, in more than 100 countries.

If prep is your bottleneck too, take it for a round.
